Spaghetti Bolognese

  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves , minced
  • 1 onion , finely chopped (brown, yellow or white)
  • 500g beef mince OR half pork, half beef
  • 1/2 cup (125 ml) dry red wine
  • 800g crushed tomato (or tomato passata)
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp white sugar
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 dried bay leaves
  • sprigs fresh thyme
  • 400 g / 13 oz spaghetti , dried
